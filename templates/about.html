About Us

We are 3rd year CSE students from JIS College Of Engineering. The idea of our project is to present a website which is able to predict whether a person is at risk of being concerned by Covid-19 or not. To do so, we use Machine Learning prediction techniques to train the computer based on actual observable outcomes made over thousands of observations stored in a ‘.csv’ file. If a person want’s to know whether he/she runs a risk of Covid-19, he/she must put in his/her data based on the parameters asked through the website. After training the machine based on previous similar data, the machine predicts the possibility of Covid-19 of that person.
